## What you may do without asking

- Use the name to **truthfully refer** to the project or unmodified software
  ("built with HilbertRaum", "compatible with HilbertRaum", "a fork of
  HilbertRaum") — nominative fair use.
- Keep the name in **unmodified copies** of the software obtained from the
  official repository, including packaging it unmodified for open-source
  software repositories and distributions (please link back to the project).
- Use the name in articles, reviews, tutorials, research, and community
  discussion.

## What requires our written permission

- Using "HilbertRaum" (or a confusingly similar name) or the logo **for a
  modified version** of the software. Forks are welcome and expressly permitted
  by the GPL — under a different name and logo.
- Selling or distributing **hardware products** (USB drives, SSDs, kits,
  preloaded devices) under the "HilbertRaum" name or logo, or in packaging
  that suggests they are official HilbertRaum products. Only official
  HilbertRaum kits carry the name.
- Using the name or logo in a **product name, company name, domain name, app
  store listing, or social media handle** that suggests affiliation with or
  endorsement by the project.
- Using the logo as your own project's or product's logo, or in modified form.

## Contact

For permission requests or questions: open an issue in this repository or
contact the maintainers (see [README](README.md)). We answer quickly and we say
yes to most community requests — the policy exists to stop misleading
commercial use, not community activity.
