Acme Remote Work Policy (Sample)

1. Eligibility
Full-time employees who have completed their probation period may request remote work.

2. Working Hours
Remote employees are expected to be available during core hours of 10:00 to 16:00 local time.

3. Equipment
The company provides a laptop. Employees are responsible for a secure internet connection.

4. Data Security
Confidential documents must be stored only on company-approved, encrypted storage.
Remote employees must not use public computers to access company systems.

5. Termination of Remote Arrangement
Either the employee or the company may end the remote work arrangement with two weeks' notice.
Repeated failure to meet availability or security requirements may result in immediate termination
of the remote arrangement.
