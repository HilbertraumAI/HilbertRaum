==============================================================================
  HilbertRaum  -  READ ME FIRST
==============================================================================

Welcome! This drive is your own private AI workspace. It runs entirely on your
computer - there is no internet connection, no account, and no cloud. Your
documents, chats, and everything you type stay on this drive.


HOW TO START
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Windows :  Double-click   "Start HilbertRaum"
  Mac     :  Double-click   "Start HilbertRaum.command"
  Linux   :  Run            "start-hilbertraum.sh"

The first time you open it, you will be asked to create a password for your
workspace. The password encrypts your workspace: your chats, your settings, and
the copies of documents you import. (The AI model files and the app itself are
not secret and stay as normal files.) Choose something you will remember -
there is no way to recover it if you forget it.


"WINDOWS PROTECTED YOUR PC"  /  "CANNOT BE OPENED"
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The first time you run an app from a USB drive, your computer may show a blue
or grey warning. This is normal for any new app and does not mean anything is
wrong. To continue:

  Windows :  Click  "More info"  then  "Run anyway".
  Mac     :  Right-click the app, choose "Open", then "Open" again.

You usually only have to do this once.


IF IT DOES NOT OPEN
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Open the "docs" folder on this drive and read "troubleshooting.md", or the
friendly walkthrough in "user-guide.md".


YOUR PRIVACY
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Nothing on this drive is ever sent anywhere. See "docs/PRIVACY.md" for the full
statement. You can unplug the drive and move it to another computer at any time
- your encrypted workspace travels with it.
